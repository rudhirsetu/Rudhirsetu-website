01

Getting Started

How to log in to Sanity Studio

  1. 1
    Open your browser and go to rudhirsetu.sanity.studio
  2. 2
    Log in with the username and password provided by your administrator.
  3. 3
    You'll see the main dashboard. The left sidebar shows all content sections — Events, Gallery Images, Donation Settings, etc.
Important: Always click "Publish" after making any changes. Without publishing, nothing will appear on the live website.
02

Managing Events

Add upcoming events and archive past ones

Adding a new event

  1. 1
    Click Events in the left sidebar.
  2. 2
    Click "Create new Event" (the + button).
  3. 3
    Fill in the details:
    • Title — Name of the event (e.g., "Blood Donation Camp at City Hall")
    • Date — Pick date and time from the calendar
    • Location — Full venue address
    • Expected Participants — Approximate attendees (optional)
    • Is Upcoming — Turn ON for future events, OFF for past events
    • Description — Any extra details about the event (optional)
  4. 4
    Click "Publish".
After an event ends: Open the event, turn OFF "Is Upcoming", update the participant count if known, then click "Publish" again.
03

Gallery Images

Upload and organise photos from events and activities

  1. 1
    Click Gallery Images in the sidebar.
  2. 2
    Click "Create new Gallery Image".
  3. 3
    Fill in:
    • Title — Short name for the photo (optional)
    • Description — What the photo shows (optional)
    • Category — Choose one:
      blood-donation eye-care cancer-awareness thalassemia-support
    • Is Featured — Turn ON to show this photo in the homepage slideshow
    • Image — Click to upload. Also fill in the "Alternative Text" box with a short description of what's in the photo
  4. 4
    Click "Publish".
Image tip: Use clear photos under 5 MB. Always choose the correct category so visitors can filter by activity type.
04

Donation Settings

Keep payment details accurate for donors

  1. 1
    Click Donation Settings in the sidebar.
  2. 2
    Edit any of the following:
    • UPI ID — e.g., rudhirsetu@upi
    • Account Name — Name on the bank account
    • Account Number — Bank account number
    • IFSC Code — Bank's IFSC code
    • Bank and Branch — Bank name and branch location
    • QR Code Image — Upload an updated QR code image
  3. 3
    Click "Publish".
Double-check all banking details before publishing. Incorrect account information can cause donors to send money to the wrong place.
05

Contact Settings

Update address, phone, email, and the Google Maps location

  1. 1
    Click Contact Settings in the sidebar.
  2. 2
    Update any of the following:
    • Address — Full office address
    • Phone Number — Contact number
    • Email — Contact email address
    • Google Maps URL — The embed link for your location (see instructions below)
  3. 3
    Click "Publish".

How to get the Google Maps Embed URL

Important — use the Embed link, not the Share link. The two look similar but are different. Follow the steps below exactly.
  1. Open maps.google.com in your browser.
  2. Search for your organisation's location and confirm it's the right pin.
  3. Click Share (the share icon near the location name).
  4. In the popup, click the "Embed a map" tab (not "Send a link").
  5. A box of code will appear that starts with <iframe src="https://www.google.com/maps/embed?...
  6. Copy only the URL inside the quotes after src= — starting from https:// all the way to the closing ". Do not copy the whole <iframe> tag.
  7. Paste that URL into the Google Maps URL field in Sanity Studio.
Example of what to paste:
https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d...

Not the full <iframe>...</iframe> block — just the URL inside it.
06

Social Media Links

Keep your social profiles up to date

  1. 1
    Click Social Media Settings in the sidebar.
  2. 2
    Update any of these links:
    • LinkedIn URL — Your LinkedIn page
    • Facebook URL — Your Facebook page
    • Instagram URL — Your Instagram profile
    • YouTube URL — Your YouTube channel
    • Description — A short message inviting visitors to follow you
  3. 3
    Click "Publish". All links update on the website automatically.
To get a link: Open the social media page in your browser and copy the full address from the address bar (e.g., https://www.instagram.com/rudhirsetu).

Best Practices

Keep your website accurate and professional

Images
  • Use clear, well-lit photos
  • Keep file sizes under 5 MB
  • Always pick the correct category
  • Fill in alternative text for every image
Text
  • Keep titles short and clear
  • Check spelling before publishing
  • Use proper capitalisation
  • Include all key details in descriptions
Events
  • Mark past events as "not upcoming"
  • Include the full venue address
  • Update participant numbers after events
  • Archive events once they're outdated
Regular Checks
  • Review contact info every month
  • Verify donation details are correct
  • Remove outdated gallery photos
  • Keep social media links current
